Usecase View

Dhara is a powerful geoscience gateway which creates a platform for geoscientist to carry out their geoscience experiments in very efficient manner. We have identified major use cases of a geoscience gateway through our literature review. Below we have listed user characteristics of identified main users of the system. 

  • Geoscientist: This actor conducts researches regarding geoscience field. Mostly this actor is attached with academic institutes like universities or other research institutes. Thus they always do researches collaboratively forming various groups.
  • Geoscience student: Unlike geoscientists, students have much simple needs. And they always look at the geoscience at a perspective of learning new things and would like to gain knowledge. 
  • Gateway developer: Gateway developers mainly engaged with developing front end and backend components of the science gateway. Their main objective is to customize the components to align with the objectives of a science community. Mainly they can be categorized into two: instance developers and framework developers. Framework developers are involved with developing back end for instance developers to customize the gateways as they wish.
  • Ordinary user: They are interested in prediction results like weather forecasting and disaster prediction. They are usually interested in the final outcome of a research in an understandable manner.


Geoscientists are core users of the geoscience gateway. According to the above characteristics of each user category, Dhara portal provides key features to achieve desired requirements of each user. Apache Rave used as the container for the Dhara portal which have interesting feature of arranging services as widgets which can added or removed from each users‟ dashboard according to their preference.
